The Queer Word




polyamory

Refers to having honest, usually non-possessive, relationships with multiple partners and can include: open relationships, polyfidelity (which involves multiple romantic relationships with sexual contact restricted to those), and subrelationships (which denote distinguishing between a ‘primary” relationship or relationships and various “secondary” relationships). post-op (also post-operative): Transsexual individuals who have attained one or more gender reassignment surgery procedures.
